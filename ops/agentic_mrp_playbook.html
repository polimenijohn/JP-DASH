Why 5 Domains?

Spreading volume across multiple domains protects sender reputation. If one domain gets flagged, the other 8 verticals keep running. Each domain handles ~80 emails/day (40 per vertical), well within safe limits for warmed domains. This is industry standard for outbound at scale.

DNS Setup — Exact Steps

For each of the 5 domains, the following DNS records need to be configured at the domain registrar (GoDaddy, Namecheap, Cloudflare, etc.).

SPF Record

Type: TXT
Host: @
Value: v=spf1 include:_spf.google.com ~all
TTL: 3600

DKIM Record

Generated automatically when you set up Google Workspace. Go to Admin Console → Apps → Google Workspace → Gmail → Authenticate Email. Google gives you a TXT record to add to DNS.

DMARC Record

Type: TXT
Host: _dmarc
Value: v=DMARC1; p=none; rua=mailto:dmarc@kashupay.com
TTL: 3600

Start with p=none (monitoring only). After 2-4 weeks of clean sending, move to p=quarantine, then p=reject.

Domain Warmup Protocol

Days 1-3
Send 5 real emails/day per domain to known contacts (team members, friends, existing partners). Get replies. This builds initial sender reputation.
Days 4-7
Ramp to 10-15 emails/day. Mix of known contacts and low-risk outreach. Continue getting replies — reply rate is the #1 signal ISPs use.
Week 2
Ramp to 20-30 emails/day. Start sending to prospect-like addresses. Monitor bounce rate (must stay under 2%) and spam complaints (must stay under 0.1%).
Week 3
Domains are warm. Hand credentials to John. Agents go live at 40 emails/day per domain. System self-manages from here.
Critical rule: Never blast a cold domain. Sending 100 emails on day 1 from a new domain will get it blacklisted instantly. The warmup takes 2-3 weeks but it's the difference between emails landing in inboxes and emails going straight to spam.

Safety & Compliance

Built-in protections:

Rate limiting: 40 emails per vertical per day, 60-180 second delays between sends. Well within safe outbound limits.

CAN-SPAM compliant: Real sender name, real business address, clear opt-out mechanism in every email.

Sending hours: 8am-5pm local time only. No weekend sends.

Bounce protection: If bounce rate exceeds 2% on any domain, that agent pauses automatically.

Domain isolation: Each domain handles only 2 verticals. If one domain gets flagged, 80% of the system keeps running.

Opt-out handling: Any unsubscribe request is immediately honored and the prospect is permanently excluded.
