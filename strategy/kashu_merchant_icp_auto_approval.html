Merchant ICP Master List

Auto-Approval (Tier I Card Present) Merchants Only
Luqra Underwriting Matrix Verified | Last Updated: March 2026

Overview

Every merchant category in this document is Tier I Card Present under the Luqra underwriting matrix. That means instant or same-day approval, no reserve addendum, no processing statements required unless AMV exceeds $150k, and FICO floor of 550. These are your fastest closes and cleanest accounts. Nothing on the restricted or prohibited list made this cut.

9 ICPs
1

Card present, high transaction volume, established processing history in most cases. One of the highest-volume merchant categories available.

2

3

5

6

9
