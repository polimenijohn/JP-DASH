What Tier II Means

These merchants are clean and boardable but require slightly more documentation than Tier I Card Present accounts. Approval is same-day (not instant). Key differences from Tier I:

Reserve Addendum required
Signer ownership over 50% must be documented
Processing statements needed if AMV exceeds $125k or avg/high ticket exceeds $1,500
Tax returns required above $125k AMV
Website must have T&Cs, privacy policy, refund policy, and secure checkout for eCommerce
FICO floor is still 550

Nothing on the restricted or prohibited list is included. These are all legitimate, scalable merchant accounts worth going after -- just expect a slightly longer boarding conversation.
