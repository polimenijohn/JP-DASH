How to Use This Doc

When recruiting an MRP, cross-reference the right column to show them exactly which of their existing clients are potential Kashu referrals. When prospecting merchants, use the MRP column to identify who already has a warm relationship with that business owner. Two birds, two stones.

Note: Primary MRP is the professional most likely to have a direct, trusted relationship with the merchant owner at the exact moment they need processing. Secondary MRP is a strong secondary channel that overlaps with the same merchant type. Both are worth recruiting independently -- but when you land the Primary MRP, every merchant in their book is a warm pipeline.
