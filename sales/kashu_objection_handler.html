"We're happy with our current processor."
I get that. Most merchants we work with said the same thing before they saw their numbers. We're not asking you to switch — we're asking you to spend five minutes to find out what you're actually paying versus what you should be paying. If the numbers don't move you, no hard feelings.
2
"I don't want the hassle of switching."
There's no downtime. You don't miss a single day of business. We handle the entire migration — equipment, gateway, everything. The only thing that changes is your statement.
3
"I'm locked into a contract."
What's the cancellation fee? Nine times out of ten, we save you more than that in the first month alone. Tell me what you're paying and I'll show you exactly when the math tips in your favor.
4
"Your rates seem too good to be true."
That's a fair reaction — most merchants have been burned before. That's exactly why we put the rate in writing before you sign anything. If we don't hit it, we pay the difference. No other processor does that.
5
"I need to talk to my business partner / accountant first."
Of course. Let me put together a one-page savings summary you can share with them. Takes the conversation out of your hands and lets the numbers do the talking. What's your email?
6
"I already got a better quote from [competitor]."
Show me the quote. I'll tell you exactly what's missing from it — because every processor has fees that don't show up in the headline number. We'll put everything in writing so you can compare apples to apples.
7
"We use Square and it works fine."
Square is convenient. But at your volume you're paying 2.6% plus $0.10 on every swipe. Kashu is 2.2% plus $0.15. On $80K a month that's roughly $300 a month walking out the door that shouldn't be. Same simplicity, better number.
8
"I don't want to change my equipment."
In most cases you keep it. We reprogram it. If there's ever a hardware issue we handle it. Nothing on your end changes except the rate.
9
"What if there's a problem?"
You have a dedicated rep who knows your account. Not a call center. Same business day response. If something ever goes wrong you have a direct line, not a ticket queue.
