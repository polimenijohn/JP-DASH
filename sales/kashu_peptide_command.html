Peptide Vertical Outreach Scripts

The Supplier Question (Onboarding / Call)

Ask every peptide merchant this during or after close. Extracts the supplier intel you need.
Hey quick question while we're getting you set up. Who do you source your peptides from? I ask because we work with a bunch of companies in this space and I like to know who the good vendors are. Sometimes I can even make intros that help both sides.

Merchant Referral Ask (Post-Onboarding)

Once the merchant is live and processing, hit them with this to get the supplier intro.
Hey [NAME], quick one for you. You mentioned you source from [SUPPLIER]. I'm working with a few other companies in the peptide space and I'd love an intro if you're open to it. Here's the thing -- for every business you connect me with that ends up processing with us, you earn residuals on their volume. Not a one-time thing. Monthly. So if your supplier has other clients in the space and they intro me, you make money every month on those accounts. Want me to send you the details?

Cold Outreach to Wholesaler (Email)

Going direct to a peptide wholesaler/vendor you've identified. Lead with value, not the ask.
Hey [NAME], I work with a bunch of peptide companies on their payment processing and I keep hearing your name come up as one of the go-to suppliers in the space. Wanted to reach out for two reasons. First, if you're processing card payments on your end, I can almost certainly save you money or add a revenue line through surcharging. I'll put it in writing before you switch anything. Second, I have a referral program that might interest you. For every business you connect me with that processes through us, you earn monthly residuals on their volume. Your clients are already my target market. You wouldn't have to sell anything. Just make an intro. Worth a quick call this week?

Wholesaler Commission Pitch (Call / Meeting)

The full pitch when you're on a call with the wholesaler. Close them on opening their client list.
Here's what I'm proposing. You have relationships with dozens, maybe hundreds of peptide companies. Every single one of them processes payments. Most of them are overpaying or using a generic processor that doesn't understand their business. I handle payment processing specifically for companies in this space. I guarantee better rates in writing. The switch takes a day. They don't miss a single transaction. What I'm offering you is simple. For every client of yours that you intro me to and they end up processing with us, you earn a percentage of the net revenue on their account. Every month. For as long as they process. You don't sell anything. You don't explain anything. You just say "hey talk to John at Kashu, he handles payments for a bunch of companies like yours." At your scale, if even 20 of your clients come on, you're looking at a few thousand a month in passive income. And that number grows as they grow. All I need is a list and a warm intro. I handle everything else.

Cold Outreach to Peptide Merchant (Email)

Direct cold outreach to peptide merchants. Pattern interrupt opener.
Hey [NAME], Most processors treat peptide companies like a risk category. I treat you like a revenue opportunity. I work exclusively with businesses in this space. I know the volume patterns, the chargeback profile, the compliance headaches. And I guarantee better pricing in writing before you switch anything. Quick example -- I just moved a company doing about $80K/month and saved them $1,400/month by adding a surcharge program. They didn't lose a single customer. They added a revenue line. Worth 10 minutes to see what the numbers look like for you?

Cold Outreach to Peptide Merchant (Text/DM)

Short version for text or LinkedIn DM.
Hey [NAME], I handle payment processing for a bunch of peptide companies. I can guarantee better rates in writing and most of my clients add a revenue line through surcharging. Worth a quick look?

Follow-Up After No Response (Wholesaler)

Bump email for wholesalers who didn't reply.
Hey [NAME], circling back on this. I just onboarded another peptide company this week and your name came up again as their supplier. The referral program is still open. Monthly residuals on any client you intro that ends up processing with us. Zero effort on your end. Happy to walk you through the numbers if you have 5 minutes this week.
